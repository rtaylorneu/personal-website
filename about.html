About

I'm an anthropologist and visual artist. My research is oriented by questions of form and force, with a focus on creative media practices.

I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at the Freie Universität Berlin, supported by the Berlin Program for Advanced German and European Studies and the Wenner-Gren Foundation. From September 2026, I will hold a SSHRC Postdoctoral Fellowship at the University of Alberta in Edmonton, Canada.
